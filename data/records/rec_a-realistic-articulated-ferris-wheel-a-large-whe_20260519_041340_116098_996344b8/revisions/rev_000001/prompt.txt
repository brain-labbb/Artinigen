A realistic articulated Ferris wheel: a large wheel with two parallel torus rims supported by an A-frame tower on each side, spinning on a horizontal axle. The wheel has spoke members, decorative inter-rim bracing, and a hub with prominent flanges. Twelve passenger gondolas hang from pivots evenly spaced around the rim; each gondola has a curved roof, an open cabin with seat plank and safety bar, and swings on its own transverse revolute pivot so it can hang upright. The base includes a platform plate, an A-frame tower on each side, cross-braced legs, and a small loading platform with a step beside the wheel.
